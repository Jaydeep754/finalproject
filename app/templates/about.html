About Us

Milk & More is a proud subsidiary of Shriji Dairy, a pioneer in the dairy industry that has been serving the community in Ahmedabad since 1994. Our Mission

To embrace the right technology to delight our customers while empowering the farming community through our unique 'relationship farming' model. We anticipate, understand, and respond to our customers' needs by creating high-quality products available through innovative channels.
