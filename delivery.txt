Authentication & Account Management

• Delivery person can login using email and password.
• Delivery person can logout securely.
• Delivery person can change password.
• Delivery person can update profile information (name, phone number).
• Delivery person can reset forgotten password.

Dashboard

• View delivery dashboard after login.
• View summary including:

Total assigned orders

Pending deliveries

Completed deliveries

Failed deliveries

Order Management

• View list of assigned orders.
• View detailed order information including:

Order ID

Customer name

Delivery address

Customer phone number

Product details

Payment method (COD / Online)

Order status

• Accept or reject assigned delivery (optional based on system design).

Delivery Status Management

• Update delivery status:

Assigned

Out for Delivery

Delivered

Failed Delivery

Cancelled

• Add delivery notes (example: customer not available).

COD Payment Handling

• Mark Cash on Delivery (COD) as collected.
• View COD collection history.
• View total COD amount collected.

Navigation & Contact

• View customer delivery address.
• View customer contact number.
• Option to call customer.
• Option to view delivery location on map (optional advanced feature).

Delivery History

• View completed deliveries.
• View failed deliveries.
• View delivery history with dates and status.

Notifications

• Receive notification when new order is assigned.
• Receive notification when order is cancelled.

Security & Access Control

• Delivery person cannot access admin panel.
• Delivery person cannot modify products.
• Delivery person cannot modify orders except delivery status.
• Delivery person can only view assigned orders.

Admin Controls (Admin Side)

Admin can:
• Add delivery person.
• Edit delivery person details.
• Delete delivery person.
• Assign orders to delivery person.
• View delivery performance.
